Who else thought the 'Divine Move' was an actual, tangible technique after watching Hikaru no Go? When I was twelve, I genuinely believed it was a legendary skill I just had to discover on my own. Honestly, I still ponder the meaning behind the concept. While the anime might not have intended anything deeply profound, I like to believe there is a beautiful underlying philosophy: that every single move played on the board contributes to the Divine Move, and the games we create together are what truly define it.
